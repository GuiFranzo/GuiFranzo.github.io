Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Guilherme Franzolin ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information when you play our mobile games.

1. Data We Collect Automatically

We do not collect "personal info" like your name or email. However, to provide ads and analyze game performance, we automatically collect:

  • IP Address and Country.
  • Device IDs (IDFA for iOS, AAID for Android).
  • Device type, Operating System, and Language.
  • In-game events (e.g., levels completed, buttons clicked).

2. Third-Party Partners (Ad Networks)

Our game uses third-party SDKs to show ads and analyze performance. These partners may collect and use your data according to their own policies:

  • Google AdMob: Privacy Policy
  • AppLovin: Privacy Policy
  • Mintegral: Privacy Policy
  • Unity Ads: Privacy Policy

3. Global Compliance

3.1 Brazil (LGPD & ECA Digital 2026)

In compliance with the Lei Geral de Proteção de Dados (LGPD) and the Digital Statute for Children and Adolescents (ECA Digital), we ensure:

  • Best Interest of the Minor: We do not perform behavioral profiling or emotional tracking of children.
  • Default Privacy: We apply the most restrictive privacy settings by default for users identified as minors.
  • User Rights: Brazilian users can request data access or deletion via the contact email below.

3.2 Europe (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation, EU users have the right to access, rectify, or erase their personal data. We use a Consent Management Platform (CMP) to collect your explicit consent before showing personalized ads.

3.3 United States (COPPA & CCPA)

  • COPPA: Our games are not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect info from them.
  • CCPA/CPRA: California residents have the right to opt-out of the "sale" or "sharing" of personal info (Device IDs). You can exercise this in our in-game settings.

4. Data Retention

We only keep data for as long as necessary to provide the service or as required by law (e.g., 6 months for certain logs under Brazilian law).
